ANTS_TAC : tactic

SYNOPSIS
Split off antecedent of antecedent of goal as a new subgoal.

DESCRIPTION
   A ?- (p ==> q) ==> r
  ======================= ANTS_TAC
   A ?- p   A ?- q ==> r

FAILURE CONDITIONS
Fails unless the goal is of the specified form.

USES
Convenient for focusing on assumptions of an implicational theorem that one wants to use.
