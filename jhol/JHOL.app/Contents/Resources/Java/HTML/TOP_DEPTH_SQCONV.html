TOP_DEPTH_SQCONV : strategy

SYNOPSIS
Applies simplification top-down to all subterms, retraversing changed ones.

DESCRIPTION
HOL Light's simplification functions (e.g. SIMP_TAC) have their traversal algorithm controlled by a ``strategy''. TOP_DEPTH_SQCONV is a strategy corresponding to TOP_DEPTH_CONV for ordinary conversions: simplification is applied top-down to all subterms, retraversing changed ones.

FAILURE CONDITIONS
Not applicable.
