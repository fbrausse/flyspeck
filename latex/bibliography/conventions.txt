% Grammatical conventions.

1. Use en-dash between coauthors: Bose--Einstein, Cayley--Menger, Riemann--Roch and
a hyphen on single names Fejes-Toth.

2. Enumerate rather than itemize.




% Word choice.  

"select" or "choose" rather than "pick":  mouse selection, natural selection, 
axiom of choice,
